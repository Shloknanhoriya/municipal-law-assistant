Property owners are required to pay property tax to the municipal corporation.

Non-payment of property tax may result in penalties or interest charges.

Municipal authorities may initiate recovery proceedings for tax arrears.

Property tax rates are determined based on property type and usage.

Late payment of taxes may attract additional fines.

Tax defaulters may face legal action under municipal laws.

Municipal bodies may issue notices for unpaid taxes.

Property tax contributes to funding civic infrastructure.

Tax exemptions may apply in specific cases as prescribed by law.

Failure to pay taxes can affect municipal service access.
