Shops and commercial establishments are required to obtain a valid trade license.

Operating a business without a trade license may attract penalties or closure notices.

Trade licenses must be renewed periodically as prescribed by municipal authorities.

Differente sellers and street vendors may require special permits.

Food-related businesses must obtain health and safety clearances.

Municipal authorities may inspect licensed premises for compliance.

Licenses may be suspended for violation of municipal conditions.

Different categories of businesses may have different licensing requirements.

Display of trade license at the business premises may be mandatory.

Licensing ensures regulation of commercial activities within municipal limits.
