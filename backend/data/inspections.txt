Municipal authorities are empowered to inspect premises for regulatory compliance.

Inspections may be conducted to verify licensing or hygiene standards.

Entry into premises may require prior notice except in urgent cases.

Unauthorized constructions may be inspected and penalized.

Health inspections may be conducted in food establishments.

Inspection reports may lead to notices or penalties.

Municipal officers must carry identification during inspections.

Inspections aim to ensure public safety and compliance.

Obstruction of inspection duties may attract penalties.

Repeated violations may result in stricter enforcement action.
